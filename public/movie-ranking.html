Matt's Favorite Movie List

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Remember The Titans

Football movie based on a true story about the Titan football team in Virginia. Addresses racism and coming together. 10/10

The Lone Survivor

True story of 4 Seals attempting to successfully take out a target. It goes wrong. 10/10

The Dark Knight

The second movie in the Christopher Nolan Batman Trilogy. Batman takes on the Joker and 2 Face to save Gotham. 10/10
